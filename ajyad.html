Welcome to Ajyaad Mother and Child Hospital

Our hospital is dedicated to providing quality healthcare services with compassion and care. We offer modern facilities, experienced doctors, and a supportive environment to ensure the well-being of our patients. Your health and safety are always our top priority.

Our Services

  • 24/7 Emergency Care: We are always open to handle urgent medical needs anytime, day or night.
  • Maternity Services: Safe deliveries, antenatal care, and postnatal support for mothers.
  • Pediatric Care: Special care and treatment for babies and children.
  • Laboratory Tests: Fast and reliable lab results to help with diagnosis.
  • Diagnostic Services: Medical tests and checkups to find health problems early.
  • Ultrasound Scans: Imaging services for pregnancy and other medical needs.
  • Circumcision: Safe and professional circumcision for children.
  • Immunization: Vaccines to protect children and adults from diseases.
  • Pharmacy: Quality medicines available at affordable prices.
  • Surgery: Skilled doctors for both minor and major surgeries.
